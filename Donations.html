Technology Donations

We need your support! We rely soley on your donations to keep us running! Working or not, all technology is useful for us. We know how to refurbish laptops and desktops, and those that can't be fixed can be used for parts. When we say "Anything is useful" keep in mind we mean ANYTHING. be assured that we will wipe all information from the device so your privacy is safe. Along with this, we will make sure to notify you when your device has found a home. Maybe if your lucky, you might get a picture of your device in its new home!

Here is a list of qualifications we request with your device:

  • Please wear a mask when giving away your device
  • Please make us aware of any data you would like us to back up for you
  • We ask that you supply us with a charger with the computer if possible
  • Please let us know of any underlying issues with the technology
