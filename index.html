Who are we?

Leemar is a team of 3 people trying to make the city of New York a better place. We are a Start Up Philanthropic group. We travel all over the city trying to aquire used technology so we can re-purpose it into equipment that people who can't afford technology can use. Our team so far is made up of three people.

Our Process

  • Collecting

    The process that we go through starts here. People will fill out a form that tells us their device and condition. We then assess their condition to see if they can be re-purposed. If this is passed, we will come and collect the technology

  • Repairing

    This process is one of the most crucial to our project. Making sure people have their data erased from their computer and making sure that we can backup everything is important to making this a smooth process.

  • Distribution

    People can complete an application to identify key issues that technology can solve for them. We will then see if we can provide a suitable device for them. If so then we will either deliver the device or they will pick it up.
